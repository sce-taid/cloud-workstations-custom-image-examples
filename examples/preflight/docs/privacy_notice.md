# Privacy Notice: Cloud Workstations Preflight

The Preflight dashboard is designed with **Privacy by Design** and **Data Minimization** as core principles.

## Data Collection Transparency

- **No PII**: We do not collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or account IDs.
- **Technical Metadata only**: The dashboard strictly utilizes operational data (Hostname, Connection Protocol, Latency, and Polling Status) to facilitate the workstation connection.
- **Local Storage**: Technical preferences (Language, Debug Mode, Timeout limits) are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted to any external server.
- **No Tracking**: No analytics cookies or tracking pixels are used within this interface.
